RJ Jewellery is the multi brand accessories store that champions independent designers and brings a sophisticated twist to classic bling. Our love for jewellery led us to create our own range that sits snugly alongside a curated selection of British accessories, all designed to be cherished and admired.

RJ Jewellery specialises in Original, Semi-Precious and Artificial Jewellery. Especially Rings, Bracelets, Necklaces and Earrings. We also have a wonderful range of giftware.

Wedding and engagement rings are available from leading brands such as Arom Basha and The Gallery, or our in-house design team can help you create the perfect bespoke ring.

With the excellent customer service expected of a family-run business, combined with modern techniques and expertise,RJ Jewellery is the perfect place for discerning customers.

We believe in the joy of giving and receiving items you can love for more than one season. Step into our online store to discover new and exciting designers that challenge convention. We love fashion, but we are not slaves to it.

We are not cheap or nasty, but we are no wallflower either.
We are RJ Jewellery.
